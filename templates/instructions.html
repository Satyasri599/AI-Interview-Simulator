Interview Instructions

  • Ensure you are in a quiet environment before starting the interview.
  • Make sure your camera is turned on during the interview.
  • Do not switch tabs or minimize the browser during the interview.
  • Each round contains different types of questions.
  • Read each question carefully before answering.
  • For MCQ questions select the correct option.
  • For theory questions type your answer clearly in the textbox.
  • The interview contains 5 rounds: Aptitude, Technical, Theory, Managerial, and HR.
  • Try to answer honestly to get accurate feedback.
  • Your final score and feedback will be displayed after completing all rounds.
Please read all instructions carefully before starting the interview.
